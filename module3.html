What is design control

Facets of Design Control

Facet

  • Design and Development Planning
  • Design Inputs
  • Design Outputs
  • Design Review
  • Design Verification
  • Design Validation
  • Design Transfer
  • Design Changes
  • Design History File
  • Risk Analysis

Description

Processes to manage the design plan, including how teams interface
The physical and performance characteristics of an Offering that are used as a basis for the Offering design (e.g., User, product, risk, and regulatory requirements)
Design Outputs are the results of the design effort and are documented and ensure adequate evaluation of conformance to Design Inputs. Design Outputs are the specifications for “how” the Design Input (requirements) will be met.
Systematic reviews at specific times of the Design Process
Confirmation by examination and provision of objective evidence that specified requirements have been fulfilled
Processes to ensure that design conforms to user needs and intended use
Processes to ensure that the design is correctly translated to the production environment
Processes for the identification, documentation, validation, review and approval of design changes
Record that lasts through the life time of the product that can demonstrate that the product was made in accordance to the Design and Development Plan and regulatory requirements
Procedure for identification, analysis, mitigation, and monitoring of risks
