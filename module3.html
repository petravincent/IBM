Systematic reviews at specific times of the Design Process
Processes to confirm that design outputs meet design inputs
Processes to ensure that design conforms to user needs and intended use
Processes to ensure that the design is correctly translated to the production environment
Processes for the identification, documentation, validation, review and approval of design changes
Record that lasts through the life time of the product that can demonstrate that the product was made in accordance to the Design and Development Plan and regulatory requirements
Procedure for identification, analysis, mitigation, and monitoring of risks
